# Generative AI : Bring AI to your Device, Simple, Secure, Sustainable 

What our team is doing: 

> By the end of the hackathon, we aim to build a practical and user-friendly
> prototype (or a tutorial) that makes local LLMs accessible and appealing to
> everyday users, while highlighting their advantages of sustainability _and_ for
> privacy and security _and_ for ethical usage.

We think that sustainable tools exist, but they are not evenly distributed. We
want to make existing gen AI tools having more awareness and more accessible.

## Our target goal

Our target goal is to strengthen AI-usage in a "responsible" way, which we
think of as a triangle of

- security/privacy
- sustainability
- ethical usage.

## Use Cases

Usually, LLMs and agentic AI address a broad use case and this is what people
are constantly amazed of. However, people are not aware of negative aspects of
using cutting-edge very large language models and a lot of use cases don't need
the broad power of these models and can be served by smaller models. Some of
the use cases are:

- audio transcription
- grammar 
- diagram generation 

We have the hypothesis that with great context for your specific use case, you
might be able to use local and smaller LLMs or a hybrid mode, which already exists.

## Our solution workflow 

We propose to provide users with a **local-first AI-gateway**, that
serves as a responsible AI tool while serving the use cases in the best way
possible.

We have two aspects to focus on (*accessibility* and *awareness*), with two
phases: *introduction* and actual *usage*:

- **Awareness aspects**
  - *Introduction Phase*: How to make everyone aware of the existence and advantages of
    the diversity of AI models (there is more than only ChatGPT and Claude) and
    the posibility of LLMs running on your local machine.
  - *Usage Phase*: How to make users of local-first LLMs be aware of responsible
    AI aspects, including sustainability?
- **Accessibility aspect**
  - *Introduction Phase*: How to make people actually install a local-first
    generative AI tool. 
  - *Usage Phase*: How to ensure that people continue to use the responsible AI
    tool, still having fun and keep being engaged.

## User Persona

We assume four user personas:

- *Petra*: A middle-aged woman who is not interested in technology or tech and
  does not know or follow the advances in Generative AI at all, even not in
  mainstream media.
- *Kai*: Kai always looks at what he can get out of tools or solutions. He
  might be aware of sustainability, but he does not take this into account when
  making decisions.
- *Daniel*: Daniel uses PCs during his work and at home, but usually is not
  interested in installing software, he just want things to work.
- *Delia*: Delia is a teenager and gamer who is impatient enough to turn of any
  tool that doesn't respond in time or keep her interested. She enjoys using
  games.
